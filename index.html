Questions
answered.

Do I need any financial background to use these? +
No. Every template has a dedicated Assumptions sheet — you just fill in your numbers in the blue cells and everything calculates automatically. If you can read a number, you can use these templates.
Do they work in Google Sheets as well as Excel? +
The templates are built and tested in Microsoft Excel. Most features work in Google Sheets when imported, though some advanced formatting and chart features may render slightly differently. Excel is recommended for the full experience.
Can I customise the templates for my business? +
Absolutely — that's the point. Rename categories, add rows, adjust assumptions. The models are built on a clean assumptions architecture so any change flows through the entire model consistently.
